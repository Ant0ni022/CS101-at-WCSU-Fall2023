Western Connecticut State University, Westside Campus Center

Western Connecticut State University, Westside Campus Center

Location: Danbury, CT
Client: State of Connecticut, Department of Construction Services (Hartford, CT)
Architect: Kevin Horn + Andrew Goldman Architects (New York, NY)
Description: General Contractor for the new Westside Campus Center, designed and built to fulfill a critical need at Western Connecticut State University.  With three residence halls situated at its Westside Campus and no central gathering place, the new Center was built to provide a sense of community for students and faculty. The Campus Center consists of a three-story, 49,000 sf building features a full-service dining area, cardio fitness center, ballroom, meeting and conference rooms, student activity space, lounge areas, and related campus and student-life facilities.
