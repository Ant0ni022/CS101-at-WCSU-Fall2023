Department of Computer Science

CS Department mission is to offer a broad and up-to-date curriculum that will prepare
students for life in this information age, and provide a curriculum and instruction that
students will find valuable in the near future.

Computer Science Major

A solid foundation in computer science, including software and hardware fundamentals.
Computer Science department focuses on providing students with those skills necessary
for an entry-level position.

Computer Science Minors

Web Development

A strong programming background in addition to other web related skills; acts as a bridge
between the Computer Science and business disciplines.

Informatics

The study of computer-based strategies for information production and retrieval; acts as a
bridge between the Computer Science and natural and physical science disciplines.

Security

A foundation in programming coupled with information systems security; acts as a bridge
between the Computer Science and Management Information Systems discipline.

Digital Media

Skills needed to organize and convey information and meaning using either still or animated.
images; acts as a bridge between Computer Science and Art disciplines
